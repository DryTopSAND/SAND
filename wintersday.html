[SAND] Wintersday Raffle

In celebration of Wintersday, there will be a raffle held at the end of December. Participants will be in the running for three prizes of gold or the grand prize:

  • Grand prize:
    Azimuth, exotic Staff with Berserker stats
    Estimated value of 50 gold
  • First Place: 25 gold
  • Second Place: 20 gold
  • Third Place: 15 gold

This raffle is a way for all of us to experience the generosity and joy of Wintersday as well as being an easy way to help build our Guild Hall a little more. We're only a couple of months into Heart of Thorns, and already we have a Lv25 Guild Hall with fully upgraded bonuses and some great vendors and synthesizers for guild members. [SAND] has truly become an amazing group of people who continue to help bring an encouraging and productive Dry Top experience to the GW2 community. It is my hope that this raffle can serve as just one of many ways for me to express my gratitude to all of you here at [SAND]. I believe that entries based on donations are the fairest way to gauge participants and it fits with the theme of Wintersday.

One thing to keep in mind

These prizes are coming from my own funds. No donations of gold from the past or present have been used to add to the pot unless the donator specifically requested it. Any gold donations from members and non-members go toward funding our Dry Top runs with trays of food and Heroes Banners, our Guild Hall, or our Guild Scribe.

Rules and Stipulations

  1. If you have donated in the past according to the below specifications going back to November 7th, you are already eligible and a participant in the raffle! This rule has been set in place to protect those who have in some cases donated hundreds of gold in items over the last month and a half.
  2. Piles of Flax Seeds totalling 25 or more and Piles of Coarse Sand totalling 50 or more count as donations.
  3. Gold donations of two (2) gold or more count as a donation.
  4. Heart of Thorns decorations and account-bound items count as a donation.
  5. You do not need to donate all at once! Your donations are tracked in a spreadsheet.
  6. Please mail Muirellthe Moon gold, Piles of Flax Seeds, or Piles of Coarse Sand. Please keep in mind that my mailbox gets full, so I may not respond right away. The Guild Treasury inside the Guild Hall will show everything we currently have and need for the Guild Hall. Please check it for what you can donate, and remember - as we unlock further upgrades and increase our Guild Hall level, more items become available as donations.
  7. The prizes will be raffled in descending order (Azimuth, 25, 20, and then 15 gold.) If you win one prize, you will no longer be eligible for another.

This raffle will officially end on December 31st at 11:59 pm PST. The winners will be decided via a random number generator and announced by January 2nd, 2016.

Those who are already presently eligible will be notified in the days ahead!

A very Merry Wintersday to all!
