Recruitment - Open! Please read and accept the information below and then PM the guild leader or an officer for an invite!

What is expected from [SAND] members:
  • Just as we aim to keep it neighborly and helpful during our events, we like it that way with our guild! Maintain a friendly and helpful attitude while representing [SAND] to those you encounter in the game and while speaking in guild chat. We believe in keeping a safe and comfortable environment for all genders, orientations, race, and religion. Our guild chat and Discord do have mature language and content. Issues of personality or opinion conflict are to be handled in a respectful manner.
  • Make use of our Guild Message of the Day, website, Twitter, and Discord to keep up-to-date with information concerning our schedules, events, and guild status. We have occasional guild meetings to help bring members informed in our Discord, though these are not mandatory to attend!
  • Keep an open mind for learning and helping out during Dry Top runs if you are participating. We believe in bringing fun, engaging, and productive Dry Top maps to the GW2 community, and our recipe is teamwork, encouragement, and communication! Muirellthe Moon and other veteran players of [SAND] are there to answer any questions you may have about events!

We have no representation or attendance policy. With that in mind, please do remember that our events are made possible through members who take the time to help out when they can!
