Recruitment - Open

What is expected from [SAND] members:
  • Maintain a friendly and helpful attitude while representing [SAND] or speaking in guild chat. We believe in keeping a safe and comfortable environment for all genders, orientations, and race. Our guild chat and TeamSpeak do have mature language and content.
  • Make use of the Guild Message of the Day, website, Twitter, and Sub-Reddit for information concerning our schedules and guild status.
  • Keep an open mind for learning and helping out during Dry Top runs if you're participating. Our success is created by all of us together. Muirellthe Moon and other veteran players of [SAND] are there to answer any questions you may have about events!
  • Remember, we have no Rep requirement, nor will we ever enforce one.
