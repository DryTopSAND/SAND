What you need to know:

  • Each Snow Pile is purchasable for 100 Snowflakes from the Guild Decoration Vendor in our Guild Hall, located near the Guild Bank. Each Snow Mound is purchasable from the same vendor for 250 Snowflakes. 1 Snow Pile = 1 Raffle Ticket. 1 Snow Mound = 2 Raffle Tickets.
  • If you donated any Snow Piles or Snow Mounds before the raffle was officially announced, they have been recorded already and will count as entries.
  • We are concentrating on Snow Piles. They are what we need to craft higher tiers of Wintersday decorations, while Snow Mounds are not used for any current Scribe decorations. While you are entirely free to donate Snow Mounds (and they are awesome!), we encourage you to consider using Snowflakes on Snow Piles more often.
  • There is a tracker in place for copying down how many Snow Piles and Snow Mounds have been donated by each person. It checks every 5 minutes and within those 5 minutes can mark down a total of 100 items. While we do not foresee anyone donating more than 100 Snow Piles/Mounds within a span of 5 minutes, we caution you to check for other people donating at the same time as you, and to slow down.
  • Each raffle ticket will be set a numerical value of however many Snow Piles have already been donated. Ex: Moon.6480 was the first to donate, and put in 10 Snow Piles in a row = raffle tickets numbered 1-10. Sand.7850 donated 3 Snow Piles in a row after, numbering them 11-13.
  • The prizes were funded by Moon and other donors. As a general courtesy and out of respect, you are not allowed to change the prize for another item(s) of an equivalent value in Gems unless you already have the prize. You will be required to submit proof that you have it. Winners can also choose to trade their prize to another [SAND] member.
  • Winners of any of the bigger prizes are eligible to win the Wintersday Earmuffs. If you have the Earmuffs already, you are free to trade them to another [SAND] member.

There is no limit to how many raffle tickets you can purchase. Winners will be randomly selected using a random number generator.

This contest officially ends January 1st at 11:59 pm (PST time), which is 7 hours and 59 minutes after reset. This is to ensure we can give out the correct prizes before they are phased out from the Gem Store.
