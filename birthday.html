That’s No Tornado [SAND] Birthday Schedule 2019

Hey folks! The time is upon us: [SAND]'s 4th Birthday!! We’re excited to celebrate our roots in Dry Top with you this year. We’ll have an open Discord, lots of chatting, trivia and more to win prizes, and, of course... T6 Dry Top! Come get those precious Geodes and get to the cheapest Dry Top vendors possible in the game with us!

🎂🎂🎂🎂

We encourage new and veteran players alike to join us for the fun. Prizes are going to be available for winning to everyone present. All we ask that you bring is a celebratory and friendly spirit, the willingness to work together, and some fun! Please never feel shy to ask questions about Dry Top or to offer a helping hand in an area! Teamwork is what Dry Top is all about, and celebrating our birthday is no exception to that truth!

The weekend of July 19-21 (Friday - Sunday)

Friday, July 19th 2019: Free day. Get a squad together, start with a world boss train, then split up where folks would like to go. Dungeons or Fractals, karaoke in Discord, merriment! Whatever folks feel like doing, excluding difficult collection or mount collection help.

Saturday, July 20th 2019:

  1. Dry Top: 11am - 2pm (PDT). Trivia and prizes included.
  2. Dry Top: 6pm - 8pm (PDT). Trivia and prizes included.

Sunday July 21st (BIRTHDAY!):

  1. Dry Top: 5pm - ??? (PDT). Drinking, trivia, and prizes included. This is our official birthday date! It’s our hope that we can enjoy at least 3 hours of T6 Dry Top.
