Spoiler Policy

Witches, Ghouls, and Mummies of all ages: we are having a Raffle! This year introduced 6 new decorations for our guild hall, and some of them require up to 150 Pumpkins! With this in mind, we wanted to give members a significant incentive to part with some of their Candy Corn! The Grand Prize is a Spooky Mount Set that has been donated to us!

10 Pumpkins are required to enter, which automatically gives you your first raffle ticket. Every 2 Pumpkins after that equals one ticket! There is no limit on how many times you can enter either, so go wild! However, we do ask that you track your own entries for us by taking a screen shot of the guild history with your donations. (We have a special log in place that tracks donations. We just ask that you keep track of your own just as a precautionary measure.) Once your screen shot is taken simply send a mail to Moon to notify her. Here's to everyone having a great Halloween, and good luck!
