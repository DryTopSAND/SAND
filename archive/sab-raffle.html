Super Adventure Box Raffle!

Grand Prize: 50 gold

Second Place: 25 gold

Third Place: To be decided

It’s the Super Adventure Box Festival! That means lots of jumping, lots of Baubles, and lots of Furniture Coins! This year [SAND] has a raffle contest to get us some more of those Super Clouds so we can stock up for future Scribe leveling and cool decorations. Our raffle contest will be carried out differently from our previous ones, so please read the rules and instructions to enter correctly!

Rules

  1. 10 Super Clouds = 1 Raffle Entry. You may enter into the raffle up to 10 times, for a total of turning in 100 Super Clouds over the course of the contest. Entry to win will be available until April 20th, when Super Adventure Box ends.
  2. You can choose to enter all at once, or spread out your entries. Muirellthe Moon will be placing your account name on a spreadsheet that will be numbered. Entries will be numbered in the order that they are accepted. Moon will be using random.org to determine the winners.
  3. Guild History for decorations goes quickly, especially with easy ones to attain like Furniture Coin tokens. Since one entry is a total of 10 Super Clouds, you will be required to take a screenshot of each of your entries of 10 Super Clouds.
  4. Your screenshots must be submitted in the form below.
  5. Our instructions and rules may seem a little complicated, and we understand it can feel like a hassle! It is done this way due to how quickly guild history can and will go during festivals where people can farm decorations consistently and in large quantities. We want to ensure that everyone gets their chance at an entry, which is why we’ve had to take extra steps to ensure no one’s entry is lost.
  6. If for whatever reason you are unable to screenshot your entry, we do have a way of finding it, but please try to help us out by screenshotting your entry first. In the link question in the form below, write "Dancira" if you cannot provide a link to your screenshot.
